# 13. Enablement & Adoption

> `Owner CoE Lead` · `Status agreed` · `Depends on Operating Model`

**Purpose** — set how people are trained, supported, and brought along.

## The approach

Build enablement as role-based learning paths delivered through a central academy, with intensity tuned
per domain (guided/low-code for self-service domains, advanced for engineering-capable ones). Support is
tiered: central platform support behind domain champions and a community of practice. Drive adoption in
phases, pulled by champions, and measure it against the strategy outcomes.

The maturity split across domains is sharp: commercial users need advanced Fabric and git skills;
finance and operations users need confident self-service in Power BI and know when to ask for help.
Run separate learning tracks from day one rather than a one-size programme. Domain champions are
identified and onboarded in wave 1 — they carry the L1 support load and accelerate local adoption.
