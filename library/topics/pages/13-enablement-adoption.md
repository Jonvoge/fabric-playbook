# 13. Enablement & Adoption

> `Owner CoE Lead` · `Status proposed` · `Depends on Operating Model`

**Purpose** — set how people are trained, supported, and brought along.

## The approach

Build enablement as role-based learning paths delivered through a central academy, with intensity tuned
per domain (guided/low-code for self-service domains, advanced for engineering-capable ones). Support is
tiered: central platform support behind domain champions and a community of practice. Drive adoption in
phases, pulled by champions, and measure it against the strategy outcomes.

## Decisions

| Decision | Options | Choice | Why | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Enablement | A1 central academy + strong support<br>A2 central academy + role-based paths; per-domain intensity<br>A3 advanced, largely self-driven per domain<br>**Other** | _proposed_ | meet teams at their maturity | proposed |
| Community & support | A1–A3 tiered: central L2/L3 + domain champions L1 + community of practice + office hours<br>**Other** | _proposed_ | scale support without a central queue | proposed |
| Change & adoption | A1 phased central<br>A2 phased + pull-based via champions<br>A3 per-domain rollout sequencing<br>**Other** | _proposed_ | adoption pulled, not pushed | proposed |
